Reimagining Placemaking・Together

Background : City Dress Up: “Seats. Together”, Hong Kong’s largest scale public art project. The project aims to inject artistic elements into the city scene of Hong Kong and promote solidarity in communities. The artworks are specifically made to echo with the environment and the characteristics of the respective locations or districts. Concept: Reimagining Placemaking・Together is a creative research project designed for City Dress Up: “Seats. Together”. Graphic designed for a creative research method to help conduct creative ideas from the users, addressing some of their problems that are not yet fully defined and shaped. The story is referenced from The Pinocchio, which explains how a puppet got lost while searching for a mysterious land. Fortunately, he was lucky enough to receive help from a little fairy who assisted him in creating a beautiful park.
