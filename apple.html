Apple Identity Design

(please note that there are no any affiliations between this work with 中条フルーツ農場.)

This design project is focused on creating an identity for an apple brand. The design is inspired by the unique features of apples, such as their iconic shape, smooth texture, and vibrant colors. The goal is to create a visually appealing graphic design that captures the essence of apples and can be applied to various mediums, including packaging and marketing materials. The design has been applied to an apple frame for sampling purposes, but there are no affiliations with any particular apple farm or brand.
