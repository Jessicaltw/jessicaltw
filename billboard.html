Billboard Webste Redesign

(please note that there are no any affiliations between this work with Cineplex.)

Billboard is an American music magazine and website that provides news, charts, and reviews of the latest music releases across all genres. Billboard covering all aspects of the music industry, from artists and albums to touring and live events.
Concept: Create a visually appealing website that reflects Billboard's nature as a leading music publication. I plan to use bold and vibrant colors, dynamic graphics, and a user-friendly layout that will make it easy for visitors to find and engage with the latest music news and content.
